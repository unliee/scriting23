ECONOMY

Cape Town is one of the most recognized business and commerce hub of the sub-Saharan region: The beautiful city contributes significantly to the economy of the whole country of South Africa. Here are a few factors that boost and improve the economy of the city;

The manufacturing Industry: Cape Town is an industrious city that manufactures assorted products ranging from refined petroleum, to wine, to citrus fruit, to textiles and clothing, to wood and wood products, to metal products and non-metalic mineral products

Tourism; As recent as from January through June 2023, Cape Town has recorded a whopping 1.4 million visitors contributing immensely to the city’s economy

Agro-Processing and Agriculture; Farming, Food processing and wineries is another source of the city’s strong and flamboyant economy

Business and Financial services; Being one of the richest cities in South Africa and also because of the two major docks and 34 berths, the city does not only attract tourists but also attracts all sorts of international and national successful businesses.

Construction; The architectural prowess of the beautiful modern city is second to none as new domestic and commercial structures are designed and constructed in the city also enhancing the economy of the city.
