ATTRACTIONS

The colorful Bo-Kaap- The collection of multi-colored houses is a sight to behold.

Table Mountain: Beautiful mountain with lot of greenery, trails, and presence of unique wild lives like rock hyrax. The table mountain is a wonderful attraction because of its flat top, hence the name, “Table Top Mountain”.

Victoria & Alfred Waterfront- Being the oldest working Habor in the southern hemisphere, This is also a great attraction.

Aquila Game Reserve Wildlife Safari- Gives the visitor that unforgettable luxurious safari experience.

Wineries and vineyards- The beautiful acres of vineyards and fantastic wineries, for all the wine lovers.

Wine is an arts that is appreciated, enjoyed and celebrated here in "Mother City" come join us all wine lovers. Cheers
