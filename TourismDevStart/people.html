PEOPLE

Cape Town being such a beautiful city it is has a number of different people from multi-racial and culturally diverse origins calling the Cape Peninsula their homes, here are a few:

Khoi People are the original and first people that inhabited the city from two thousand years ago, therefore the majority of the population of the beautiful city are blacks.

For the Dutch East India Company, Jan Van Riebeeck developed a refreshment station here in Capetown back in the 15th century,A significant amount of the white/Caucasians settled in the area thus another significant population of the city are white/Caucasians.

There is the coloured people, these are people of mixed racial backgrounds as a results of the interbreeding of white settlers and the black inhabitants.

There is also the Indian/Asian population whos ancestors were transported as labourers to work on the sugarcane plantations of Natal Colony.
