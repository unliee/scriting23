ABOUT

Come join us in our beautiful city. A port city situated at the northern end of the Cape Peninsula, where the Atlantic and Indian oceans blend. A sublime of 72 natural white sand beaches, crystal clear-water, 130 majestic mountains decorating the sky horizon. Mediterranean weather with mild winters and hot summers.

The city of Cape Town is the legislative capital of South Africa covering a total land area of 2,446 square kilometers of diverse beauty and all sorts of exhilarating activities in various locations. It is impossible to not find something for everyone in this dreamy city of Cape town. The following mentions some of the most iconic thrills the city has to offer; Table Mountain Aerial cable, Victoria and Alfred water front, Lions head, Ocean Aquarium, Clifton Beaches, The colorful Bo-kaap, Aquila Game Reserve Wildlife Safari, whale watching, Champagne Cruise, Ocean Safari.
