About

Ifeanyi Kenneth Nzubechi is an international student currently enrolled to the Multimedia production at Lethbridge College. He has tried his hand on all media related crafts and he is convinced that media art is his life. An artist at heart, his devout love and respect for arts has stopped him from officially kicking off his career as a film/music writer, producer and director until he has properly acquired the professional skills to enable him to properly convey and communicate his creativity. Kenneth was born and raised in Nigeria, a country with heavy presence of storytelling and a robust history in general. Due to his vivid imagination, He was influenced greatly by movies and also physical events that happened around him growing up. After secondary school, Kenneth apprenticed in a bookshop in the capital city of Nigeria; Abuja, where he was a bookkeeper and salesman from 2014 to 2019. He considers period to be one of the greatest blessings of his life because most times driven by love and passion, and some other times out of sheer boredom.
