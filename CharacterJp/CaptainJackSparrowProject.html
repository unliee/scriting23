ABOUT

Captain Jack Sparrow is one of the 9 Pirate lords of the seven seas.
He is notoriously known for his extreme brilliance in the craft of being a pirate in the Caribbeans back in the 18th century.

Captain Jacksparrow is a fictional character developed by Ted Elliott and Terry Rossio, however the complete version of the character was modified by Johnny Depp who also portrayed the in the movie; "Pirate Of The Caribbeans".

PERSONALITY AND HABITS

Captain Jacksparrow even though a pirate of the 18th century stands out significantly when compared to other pirates of his time. These few qualities are some of the obvious traits of Captain Jacksparrow:

  • Captain Jacksparrow like other pirates is fascinated by the adventure that comes with sailing the oceans but he perceives the whole thing differently; he appreciates the freedom that having a ship can give you in the 1700s.
    This is the reason why Jack will prefer to go in search of lost treasures rather than robbing and pillaging others like tradintionaly pirate of the time.

  • Captain Jack is the physical personification of self-interest prioritized.
  • He is extremely witty, charismatic and most times, morally gray
  • A stickler of the Pirate Codes; when he wants to.
  • A Drunkard.
  • Never barbaric or violent, naturally will try to escape an unsafe situation and will only resort to fighting when it is absolutely necessary.

KNOWLEDGE AND SKILLS

Captain Jacksparrow is one of, if not the best ship captain in his era. Jack endowed with his natural brilliance and wittiness and unique compass; his skill of navigating the ocean is extremely topnotch

Captain Jack has gret communication skills, user of facncy words and unorthodox combination of phrases that is designated to throw others off

Another great skill that The Captain has is his negotiating skills, Jack can and will negotiate himself out of any given situation, be it escaping an execution to convincing his crew members to take the craziest instruction.

The Captain is an excellent swordsman and marksman as well.
He efficiently combines his ability to use the sword, his pistol and other general improvised tools at his disposal in the fighting scene to his advantage.
